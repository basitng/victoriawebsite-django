About Our Church

WE LIVE TO PRAISE HIM
Welcome to Victory Tabernacle International Ministries Inc., where our vision is to reach the lost with the message of love, acceptance and victory through Jesus Christ.

We are committed to the teaching of the Word of God with simplicity and understanding. Our passion is to help people from all walks of life to discover their value and potential in God and equipping them to effectively reach others. We are glad that you want to be a part of what God is doing through our ministry.
